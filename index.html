Welcome

I am currently an Assistant Professor in School of Business and Management, Shanghai International Studies University. I graduated with a Ph.D. in Strategic Management from Drexel University in 2019.

My research focuses on understanding how individuals affect and are affected by entrepreneurship and technology innovation. In my recent projects I look at:

  • Hybrid entrepreneurship
  • Learning-by-hiring
  • Awards and scientific productivity
